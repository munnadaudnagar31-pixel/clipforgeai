1. Who We Are

ClipForge AI ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered gaming highlight clip generation service. Our platform allows streamers and content creators to automatically extract highlight clips from Twitch VODs, YouTube streams, and uploaded video files.

For the purposes of GDPR, ClipForge AI acts as the data controller for personal data collected through our platform.

Contact: privacy@clipforge.ai

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Providing the service — Processing your video files, generating clips, and delivering them to you
  • Authentication — Verifying your identity when you log in
  • Billing — Managing your subscription and processing payments via Stripe
  • Communication — Sending clip-ready notifications, usage warnings, and (only with consent) product updates
  • Improving the service — Analysing aggregate, anonymised usage patterns to improve our AI models
  • Security — Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorised access

We do not use your video content to train AI models without your explicit opt-in consent.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use a minimal set of cookies:

  • Session cookie — Keeps you logged in (essential, expires in 30 days)
  • Preferences — Your dashboard settings stored in localStorage (never sent to our servers)

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels, or share data with ad networks. We do not use Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel.

6. Data Retention

  • Raw video files — Deleted from our servers within 7 days of processing
  • Generated clips — Stored for as long as your account is active; deleted 30 days after account deletion
  • Account data — Retained until you delete your account
  • Billing records — Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Server logs — Retained for 90 days, then automatically purged

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California), you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Rectification — Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — Delete your account and all associated data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — Export your clip library in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction — Ask us to stop processing your data while a dispute is resolved
  • Object — Opt out of marketing emails at any time via Settings → Notifications

To exercise any right: email privacy@clipforge.ai or use the Delete Account button in Settings. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.2+
  • Data at rest encrypted with AES-256 (AWS S3 server-side encryption)
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • OAuth tokens stored encrypted in the database
  • API access controlled via JWT with short expiry (1 hour)
  • Infrastructure access restricted by IP allowlist and MFA

If you discover a security vulnerability, please email security@clipforge.ai. We operate a responsible disclosure policy.

9. Children's Privacy

ClipForge AI is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at privacy@clipforge.ai.

10. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or a prominent notice in the app at least 14 days before the change takes effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent version.

11. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights:
